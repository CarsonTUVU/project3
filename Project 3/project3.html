About Me

Hello! My name is Carson Thorpe and I have decided to make this website to increase awareness of emotional intelligence. I feel like in this world of constant distractions it can be easy to ignore our emotions which could lead to us being controlled by our emotions without us even realizing it. I recently realized that I was suffering from just that. Whenever I would feel an uncomfortable emotion I would quickly try to extinguish it by scrolling on my phone or playing video games. Understanding our emotions and developing healthy coping strategies, I believe, is one of the most important things for people in this ever increasingly reactionary world. We need to recognize why we are reacting in such a way so we can control ourselves. Here is a video on emotional intelligence if you wish to learn more:
