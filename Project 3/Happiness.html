How Are You Feeling?

Happy

A sense of joy or pleasure.

Sad

The feeling of sorrow or disappointment.

Angry

A response to frustration, injustice, or perceived wrongs.

Suprised

A reaction to the unexpected, which can be either positive or negative.

Fearful

Looking forward to a future event or outcome.

Disguested

A feeling of repulsion, often toward something offensive or distasteful.
